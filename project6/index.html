Considering a Computer?

Computers are a part of everyday life, and it has been a requirement for many in this evolving world. Whether it's for simple education usage to complex business needs, there is many types of computers and each one has their own purposes. In this page, we will discuss some type of computers you can expect to see that satisfies certain tasks.

Different Types of Computers

A desktop is considered the basic consumer personal computer. These are the ones that you can buy on your own in a brick-and-mortar store, such as Best Buy or Micro Center.

Servers are more professional-grade computers that are tailored for bigger companies dealing with complex tasks.

Mainframes are powerful computers used for large-scale computing tasks, such as processing bulk data or transactions.
